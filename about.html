Who We Are

Meet Steve & Ryan

Volta Electrical was founded in 1999 by Steve Hardy, a fully qualified electrician with a passion for doing things properly. After 20 years building a reputation across Greater Manchester, Steve was joined by his son Ryan in 2019 — fresh from his apprenticeship and full of energy for the trade.

Together they run a tight, honest operation. No subcontractors, no cutting corners. When you book Volta Electrical, Steve or Ryan will show up — and they will leave your home cleaner than they found it.

They are NICEIC registered and Part P certified, which means every job is done to the highest safety standards and fully compliant with UK building regulations.
