We handle everything from survey to installation to certification, leaving you with a fully compliant system and complete peace of mind.

Full Rewires

If your property was built before 1980 and has never been rewired, chances are the wiring is due for replacement. Old rubber-insulated cables degrade over time and can become a serious fire hazard. A full rewire brings your home up to current safety standards and gives you the capacity for modern electrical demands.

We know rewiring sounds disruptive, but we work room by room to minimise mess and get you back to normal as quickly as possible. All our rewires come with a full test certificate and are registered with your local building control.

Full test and certification
Building control registered

Landlord EICR Certificates

Since April 2021, landlords in England are legally required to have an Electrical Installation Condition Report (EICR) for their rental properties. This must be done before new tenancies begin and at least every five years thereafter.

Our qualified inspectors will thoroughly test your property's electrical installation and provide a detailed report. If any issues are found, we can carry out the necessary remedial work to bring everything up to standard — all in one visit where possible.
